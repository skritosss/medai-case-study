# Privacy And Safety Notes

Medical and health-related products require a higher standard of care than ordinary productivity software. This public repository intentionally avoids exposing private implementation details.

## Public Repository Rules

- Do not publish private product source code.
- Do not publish clinical logic or rules.
- Do not publish private datasets.
- Do not publish personal, partner, or otherwise sensitive information.
- Do not publish sensitive screenshots.
- Keep examples abstract and non-operational.

## Product Safety Principles

- AI should support professional review, not replace it.
- Missing or uncertain information must be visible.
- Outputs should be explainable at the workflow level.
- Escalation paths should be part of the product design.
- Auditability matters for trust and accountability.

## Portfolio Purpose

This repository exists to demonstrate product thinking, architecture awareness, and responsible handling of sensitive work. It is not a medical tool, not medical advice, and not a production product release.
